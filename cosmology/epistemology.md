Surmounting this requires mediated extraction. LAP1-B’s observability depends entirely on its location behind the gravitational lensing cluster MACS J0416.1-2403. Furthermore, the epoch of reionization acts as an erasure operator that paradoxically preserves. Thus, the rarest fossils are preserved by the very radiation that sought to sterilize them, provided they can be recovered through geometric mediation.
